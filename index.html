Global Volcanic Eruption

Throughout the years, volcanic eruptions happening all around the world have affect many lifes.

Population living nearby volcanoes have a very high risk of being victims in an eruption.

This could lead to many deaths, injuries, power outages, contaminations and lost of properties.

In this visualisation, we will dive in and take a look on the different eruptions which happened

around the world thoughout the years and its characteristics and statistics.
